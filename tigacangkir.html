Our Story

A Sanctuary for Coffee Lovers

TIGACANGKIR is more than just a coffee shop; it's a sanctuary for those who appreciate the finer details of the bean. We combine ethically sourced, high-quality beans with a warm, sophisticated atmosphere to create the perfect urban retreat.

Every cup tells a story of craftsmanship, from the precise roast profile to the delicate pour. Whether you're here for an early morning boost or a reflective evening sip, we provide the space for moments that matter.
